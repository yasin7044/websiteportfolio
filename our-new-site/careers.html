Content Writer

We are looking for a Content Writer to join our team and develop content for our marketing team including new blog posts or our websites, email blasts, newsletters etc. Content Writer responsibilities include conducting thorough research on industry-related topics, generating ideas for new content and proofreading and editing articles before publication. You’ll be expected to deliver quality writing pieces that appeal to our audiences, attract customers and boost brand awareness.

  • Have a customer centric approach to writing, understand concepts and build stories around it
  • Able to manage a content calendar and continuously produce content for web pages, blogs, social media posts and emailers
  • Identify customers’ needs and gaps in our content and recommend new topics
  • Research on industry topics and create blogs
  • Ensure all-around consistency in the content
