Almost half of India’s population work in agriculture, yet they constitute only 17% of the country’s GDP. In addition to lower average income, Indian farmers face many challenges. Learn more about the issues and how Utkal helps.

Utkal Tubers is a Bangalore based agri-tech company that targets the availability of high quality early generation truthfully labelled potato seeds to farmers catering to the table and processing markets in India. We are one of the leading suppliers of G3 seed potatoes in India.

Since our inception in 2016, we have built capacity to produce and market leading domestic varieties through a network of tissue culture labs, multiplication facilities for tuber production, controlled farming operations, state specific distribution networks and requisite management.

We plan to develop an end-to-end engagement model with farmers, from contracting farm tissue tubers until the harvest of pre-commercial seeds for subsequent sale in the Indian subcontinent, Africa, Middle East and South East Asia.

Our Buyback Model

Utkal provides seed potatoes to farmers in exchange for the crops with a guarantee to buy them back at a locked-in price. This model protects the farmers from price fluctuations in the market, ensuring their financial security.

Where We Are

Utkal Tubers is the first company to successfully grow seed potatoes in Gujarat and Assam, improving seed availability and making the previously expensive seeds affordable to farmers in these regions.

We are in collaboration with IPM Potato group, Ireland, an international leader in potato variety innovation to bring the best potato varieties not only to India but all around the world.
