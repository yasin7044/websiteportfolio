About Us

We are a young food brand providing you the best of Desi Cuisine at really affordable rates. We mainly focus on Indian vegetarian meals, snacks, sweets, and beverages and have created a spread of dishes to be relished by the palate.

Mission

To be known as a one stop solution entity which serves food that exceeds customer expectations in relation to taste, quality dining, hygiene and affordability.

Commitment

We create and maintain product and services of highest achievable standards. We offer our products and services at an affordable price point. We motivate and support members of Kaafi Desi team at all levels, fueling continuous growth.

“Essentially we are a food brand providing great taste at an affordable cost for our growing set of customers”

What We Do

Dine-in

Our main store outlet is located in Kundalahalli, where we offer an extensive menu that runs through breakfast till dinner. We offer affordable and home-like meal service for our regular and growing customer base.

Pop-Up

Pop-up food stalls are another way for us to reach our prospective customers and give them a taste of what we offer. We have conducted Pop-ups at corporate events, residential complex functions as well as festive meals. Our major offerings at these events are a variety of snacks, chaats, sweets and beverages

Private Consummates

From birthday parties to wedding, anniversaries to ethnic festive celebrations, we guide you through food selection, concept that meet your requirements for budget and palate. This includes both off-site and on-site preparation, for which we offer a range of set menus to choose from and also customize it as per client preferences. Currently we have a capacity to cater up to 800 people at an event and we’re working on increasing this in coming future
