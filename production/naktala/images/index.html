About Us

A known Durga Puja Organisation Where 4,00,000 people visit every year.

The club has started in the year 1986. The local families in this are formed the club local get-together. The club was famous for it kali Puja. On the year 1986, the club started the Durga Puja in a traditional & homely manner. With involvement of Puja increased the participation of people. This also increased the information sharing of people of the area to know the cultural depth of the people. The club is also very active in social activities. They have been very active in helping Thalesemia Patients. They have helped 5 patient families to run the treatment & cost of medicines. The Puja initially was in the traditional manner in which deity wad also in the traditional form. The club started to organize the theme Puja from 2003.
