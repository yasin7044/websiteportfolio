THE TEA BREWING's Shipping Policy
We efforts to deliver products purchased from ''THE TEA BREWING'' in excellent condition and in the shipping fastest time possible.
Shipping Within India
For all purchases of Rs. 499 or more, we will deliver the order to your doorstep free of cost. A shipping charge of Rs. 50 will be applicable to all orders under Rs. 499.
If the order is cancelled, lost or un-delivered to your preferred location, we will refund the complete order amount including any shipping charges, if paid online.
Shipping Outside India

For all purchases of USD or more, we will deliver the order to your doorstep free of cost. A shipping charge will be applicable as follows on orders below USD 100: Less than 55 USD - Delivery charge of USD 30 56 - 99 USD - Delivery charge of USD 20.

If the order is cancelled, lost or un-delivered to your preferred location, we will refund the complete order amount including any shipping charges, if paid online.

While we try to get the shipment out as soon as possible, however, it may take two working days to process your order.

Please note that we do not accept cancellations once the orders have been dispatched from our fulfillment center.

Delivery Partners

For international shipping, we work primarily with DHL/FedEx, EMS (India Post) and Air Mail to deliver our teas worldwide. Express service (DHL/FedEx) takes up to 6-8 working days for delivery, EMS Speed Post takes up to 30-45 working days for delivery & Air Mail takes up to 45-60 days for delivery.

For shipping to destinations within India, we primarily work with Bluedart,DTDC,EMS(India Post) . We use EMS for Out of Delivery Areas(ODA).
